Project 1: Bootstrap Site

In this project, you'll demonstrate your ability to manipulate a Bootstrap template in order to make a simple website.

Feel free to be creative, and pay attention to details. Any work you're putting on your GitHub repo should be something you're comfortable showing a potential employer. It need not be technically amazing, but it should be complete and without typos.

Instructions

  1. Start with the Business Casual template at https://startbootstrap.com/template-categories/full-websites/; download it, upload it into your projects folder, unzip it, and rename the top level folder 1. (The home page should be available at <your GitHub pages url>/it202/projects/1/index.html .)
  2. Come up with a real or imaginary company for the content, and replace the images and the text; you can either upload image files to your folder, or you can make absolute references to files on the web.
  3. Check your page(s) in the HTML validator.
  4. Save and push to your GitHub repo.

Evaluation

The evaluation and scoring will be based on the completeness of the content replacement; 25% for each of the four pages.
