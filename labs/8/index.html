Lab 8: Basic Service Worker

In this lab, you'll get a little practice with Service Workers, enabling off-line capability, and enabling native device integration.

Instructions

  1. Create a /labs/8/ folder in your workspace.
  2. Work through the CodeLab Adding a Service Worker and Offline into your Web App linked below; you'll run into several places you'll need to modify URLs because the CodeLab assumes you're working at the top level of your domain.
  3. Once your app is working, navigate to step 8 of the CodeLab Your First Progressive Web App.
  4. Follow the instructions, and create a manifest.json file, updating the text as needed for this app. You'll also need to create or copy images for your app and update the manifest with the correct references.
  5. Push to GitHub.
